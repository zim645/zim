About Us

The Zimbabwe Organization for Scientific Research is dedicated to promoting scientific inquiry and innovation within Zimbabwe. Founded in 2025, our organization brings together researchers, academics, and industry professionals to address the unique challenges facing our nation through collaborative research initiatives.

We aim to collaborate with local and international partners to enhance research capabilities and foster a culture of scientific exploration. By supporting Zimbabwean scientists and providing resources for cutting-edge research, we strive to contribute to the sustainable development of our country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support scientific research and innovation that addresses the unique challenges facing Zimbabwe. We strive to empower researchers and institutions to work together towards sustainable solutions for our communities.

Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence

Developing AI-driven solutions for industries such as healthcare, agriculture, and finance.

Renewable Energy

Researching solar, wind, and hydro energy systems to provide sustainable power solutions.

Biotechnology

Advancing genetic engineering and biopharmaceuticals to improve health and agriculture.

Data Science

Leveraging big data and analytics to solve complex problems in various sectors.

Space Science

Exploring satellite technology and space exploration for national development.

Environmental Science

Studying climate change, conservation, and sustainable resource management.

STEM Education

Promoting science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education at all levels.

Robotics

Developing robotic systems for industrial automation and healthcare applications.

Nanotechnology

Researching nanomaterials for use in medicine, electronics, and energy storage.

Technopreneurship Training

Empowering innovators with skills to transform ideas into successful tech-based businesses.

Software Development

Building scalable and innovative software solutions for local and global markets.

Quantum Technology

Exploring quantum computing, cryptography, and communication for future innovations.
