About Us

The Zimbabwe Organization for Scientific Research is dedicated to promoting scientific inquiry and innovation within Zimbabwe. Founded in 2025, our organization brings together researchers, academics, and industry professionals to address the unique challenges facing our nation through collaborative research initiatives.

We aim to collaborate with local and international partners to enhance research capabilities and foster a culture of scientific exploration. By supporting Zimbabwean scientists and providing resources for cutting-edge research, we strive to contribute to the sustainable development of our country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support scientific research and innovation that addresses the unique challenges facing Zimbabwe. We strive to empower researchers and institutions to work together towards sustainable solutions for our communities.

Research Areas

Sustainable Agriculture

Developing drought-resistant crops and sustainable farming practices to ensure food security.

Renewable Energy

Researching solar, hydro, and other renewable energy sources to power rural communities.

Public Health

Addressing disease prevention and improving healthcare delivery systems.

Water Management

Finding solutions for clean water access and conservation to combat water scarcity.

Agriculture Sector

Crop Innovation

We focus on developing high-yield, drought-resistant crops to improve food security and support farmers in challenging climates.

Sustainable Practices

Promoting eco-friendly farming techniques such as conservation agriculture, crop rotation, and organic farming.

Agri-Tech Solutions

Leveraging technology like drones, IoT, and AI to optimize farming processes and increase productivity.

Farmer Training

Providing education and resources to farmers to enhance their skills and adopt modern agricultural practices.

Market Access

Connecting farmers with local and international markets to ensure fair pricing and economic growth.

Soil Health

Researching soil conservation and fertility management to ensure long-term agricultural sustainability.

Engagement

Institutions

We partner with universities, research institutions, and government bodies to drive scientific progress and policy development.

Technology

We promote the adoption of cutting-edge technologies to solve local challenges and improve livelihoods.

Employment

We create opportunities for scientists, engineers, and innovators through research projects and collaborations.

Trading

We facilitate the commercialization of research outcomes, fostering economic growth through science-based enterprises.
