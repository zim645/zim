The Problem We Solve

⚠️ Before BPH

  • Farmers travel over 100 km to Hwange or Bulawayo
  • High chick mortality during long-distance transport
  • No local training or veterinary support
  • No hardy breeds available for hot climate
  • Women and youth face barriers due to travel costs

✅ After BPH

  • Chicks available locally — minutes away, not hours
  • Mortality significantly reduced with 4-week-old growers
  • Practical training delivered at village level
  • SASSO breed — heat-tolerant & disease-resistant
  • Special support and outreach for women and youth groups

Our Core Operations

🐣 Solar Hatchery

Produces day-old SASSO chicks and 4-week-old growers. Solar-powered — reliable even during grid outages.

Output: Expanding to serve hundreds of farmers monthly.
Advantage: 4-week-old growers are hardier, with lower on-farm mortality.

🏪 Agricultural Input Shop

One-stop shop for smallholder farmers in Binga District.

  • Chicken feeds (starter, grower, layer)
  • Vaccines and veterinary medicines
  • Maize seeds and fertilizers
  • Feeders, drinkers, brooding equipment

📚 Training & Extension

Practical, science-based training delivered in local languages (Tonga, Ndebele, English).

  • Village-based learning sessions
  • Demonstration flocks
  • Take-home checklists and picture guides

Our Breed: SASSO — Built for Binga

🌡️ Heat toleranceThrives in temperatures up to 35°C — ideal for Binga's climate
🛡️ Disease resistanceBred specifically for low-input tropical farming systems
🍗🥚 Dual-purposeExcellent for both meat production and egg laying
🌾 Low-input foragingNaturally forages for food, reducing feed costs
🍖 Meat qualityYellow-skinned, firm texture — preferred in premium markets

✅ SASSO genetics are developed by Hendrix Genetics specifically for free-range, tropical environments.

Farmer Training Program

🐤 Brooding Management

Temperature control, litter management, day-old chick care, and health monitoring. The single most critical phase for chick survival.

💊 Health & Biosecurity

Newcastle disease vaccination, recognizing sick birds, basic hygiene protocols, and preventing disease spread.

🌽 Local Feed Formulation

Using maize, sunflower cake, and locally available ingredients to reduce feed costs while maintaining nutrition.

📝 Record Keeping

Simple paper-based or phone-based records for mortality, weight gain, and feed consumption.

Contract Farming Pilot

BPH is launching a smallholder contract farming (out-grower) pilot for committed farmers in Binga District.

  • BPH provides: SASSO chicks, feed, vaccines, and technical support
  • Farmer provides: Secure housing, water, daily care, and follows training protocols
  • BPH guarantees: Market for all healthy birds at harvest time
  • Performance bonus: Additional payment for farmers with high survival rates
  • Focus: Women and youth farmers are prioritized for pilot participation

Climate-Resilient Features

☀️ Solar-Powered Operations

Hatchery runs on solar energy — no dependence on unreliable grid power. Low carbon footprint.

🐔 Heat-Tolerant Breeds

SASSO chickens are specifically selected for hot, dry conditions common in Binga District.

💧 Water-Efficient Training

Farmers learn water conservation techniques for both poultry and feed crops.

🌾 Local Feed Sources

Promoting drought-tolerant feed crops (sorghum, millet) and reduced dependence on imported feed.
