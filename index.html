Bulawayo City Parking By-laws & Regulations

Tendy Three Investments operates in full compliance with the City of Bulawayo's parking regulations. Understanding these by-laws ensures a smooth and penalty-free parking experience.

Key By-laws for Motorists:

  • **Payment Obligation:** All vehicles parked within designated Tendy Three parking zones during operational hours (6 AM - 7 PM, Mon-Sat) must have a valid and paid parking session or pass.
  • **Proper Parking:** Vehicles must be parked within marked bays and not obstruct traffic, pedestrian flow, or emergency access.
  • **Prohibited Areas:** Parking is strictly prohibited in yellow-lined areas, bus stops, taxi ranks, loading zones (unless authorized), and fire hydrant zones.
  • **Infringement Penalties:** Failure to comply with by-laws may result in fines, clamping, or towing of your vehicle, as per **SI 220 of 2023**.

**Official Document:** For comprehensive details, please refer to the official document:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find quick answers to common questions about Tendy Three Investments parking services in Bulawayo.

Payments

How do I pay for parking using EcoCash/OneMoney/MyCash?

Simply approach a Tendy Three parking marshal. They will guide you through the process, which involves dialing a USSD code or using a merchant code, and entering your vehicle registration number and the amount. You will receive an SMS confirmation.

Can I extend my parking remotely?

Yes, you can extend your parking session using the same mobile money methods. Ensure you use the same vehicle registration number. Details will be on your initial payment confirmation SMS.

Passes

How does the Daily Pre-paid Pass work?

For just US$4, the Daily Pre-paid Pass allows you unlimited parking in any Tendy Three managed bay in the Bulawayo CBD for the entire operational day (6 AM - 7 PM). The pass is linked to your vehicle's registration number, so no physical ticket is required.
