We are

Inspiring and Helping for Better Lifestyle

As a local non-governmental organization, we are deeply committed to promoting the social, educational, and economic empowerment of youths and vulnerable children in marginalized communities. Through innovative programs, we strive to break down barriers and create pathways to success. At Rise and Shine, we believe that when young people rise, entire communities thrive. Join us in building a more equitable and inclusive society.

Our Core Aim: Promoting a better quality of life for youths and vulnerable children in marginalized communities.

Vision and Mission

Rise and Shine Empowerment Center

Our Vision

We envision a society where every youth and vulnerable child from marginalized communities has the opportunity to reach their full potential and participate equally in all aspects of life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the educational, social, and economic empowerment of youths and vulnerable children through skills training, safe shelter, and access to technology.

Our Core Objectives

I

Empower Youth Through Skills

To empower youth in marginalized communities through sports, technical and vocational skills training for sustainable livelihoods.

II

Enhance Education Access

To enhance access to quality education and learning resources for vulnerable children in marginalized communities.

III

Provide Safe Shelter

To provide safe and secure shelter and care for vulnerable children and youth at risk.
