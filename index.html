About Binga

Binga is a captivating district nestled on the southeastern shores of the vast Lake Kariba in Matabeleland North Province, Zimbabwe. It's a place where the rich cultural heritage of the Tonga people thrives amidst stunning natural landscapes.

Known for its vibrant fishing industry, traditional villages, and unique geological features, Binga offers an off-the-beaten-path adventure. Visitors can immerse themselves in local customs, enjoy the tranquility of the lake, or explore the area's hidden gems.

From the famous Binga Hot Springs to the intricate basketry of the Tonga craftsmen, every corner of Binga tells a story waiting to be discovered.
