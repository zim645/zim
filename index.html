📖 Who We Are

Binga Poultry Hub (BPH) Limited is a startup social enterprise based in Binga District, Matabeleland North, Zimbabwe. We are an integrated poultry production and agricultural input supply business serving smallholder farmers.

Our model combines a solar-powered hatchery, an agricultural input shop, and practical farmer training. We focus on climate-resilient practices and prioritize women and youth entrepreneurs.

Our mission: To eliminate the agricultural input supply gap in remote Binga District by providing affordable, high-quality day-old chicks, point-of-lay pullets, and supplies locally, while empowering smallholder farmers through science-based training and guaranteed market access.

Our Core Operations

🐣 Solar Hatchery

Produces day-old SASSO chicks and 4-week-old growers. Solar-powered — reliable even during grid outages.

Output: Expanding to serve hundreds of farmers monthly.
Advantage: 4-week-old growers are hardier, with lower on-farm mortality.

🏪 Agricultural Input Shop

One-stop shop for smallholder farmers in Binga District.

  • Chicken feeds (starter, grower, layer)
  • Vaccines and veterinary medicines
  • Maize seeds and fertilizers
  • Feeders, drinkers, brooding equipment

📚 Training & Extension

Practical, science-based training delivered in local languages (Tonga, Ndebele, English).

  • Village-based learning sessions
  • Demonstration flocks
  • Take-home checklists and picture guides

Our Breed: SASSO — Built for Binga

🌡️ Heat toleranceThrives in temperatures up to 35°C — ideal for Binga's climate
🛡️ Disease resistanceBred specifically for low-input tropical farming systems
🍗🥚 Dual-purposeExcellent for both meat production and egg laying
🌾 Low-input foragingNaturally forages for food, reducing feed costs
🍖 Meat qualityYellow-skinned, firm texture — preferred in premium markets

✅ SASSO genetics are developed by Hendrix Genetics specifically for free-range, tropical environments.

Farmer Training Program

🐤 Brooding Management

Temperature control, litter management, day-old chick care. The most critical phase for survival.

💊 Health & Biosecurity

Newcastle vaccination, recognizing sick birds, basic hygiene protocols.

🌽 Local Feed Formulation

Using maize, sunflower cake, and local ingredients to reduce feed costs.

📝 Record Keeping

Simple paper or phone-based records for mortality, weight, and feed.

Contract Farming Pilot

BPH is launching a smallholder contract farming (out-grower) pilot for committed farmers in Binga District.

  • BPH provides: SASSO chicks, feed, vaccines, technical support
  • Farmer provides: Secure housing, water, daily care, follows training
  • BPH guarantees: Market for all healthy birds at harvest
  • Performance bonus: Additional payment for high survival rates
  • Focus: Women and youth prioritized for pilot
