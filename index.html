Core Aims & Objectives

ORGANIZATION AIM

Promoting a better quality of life for youths and vulnerable children in marginalized communities.

Core Objectives

I

Youth Empowerment

Core Objective

To empower youth in marginalized communities through sports, technical and vocational skills training, leadership development, and life skills education.

Programs include:

  • Sports academies and competitions
  • Vocational training workshops
  • Leadership and entrepreneurship programs
  • Life skills and mental health support
II

Quality Education Access

Core Objective

To enhance access to quality education for vulnerable children in marginalized communities through scholarships, learning centers, and educational support programs.

Programs include:

  • After-school tutoring and homework clubs
  • Scholarship programs for primary and secondary education
  • Digital literacy and computer skills training
  • School supplies and uniform assistance
III

Safe & Secure Shelter

Core Objective

To provide safe and secure shelter for vulnerable children through residential care facilities, emergency housing, and family support services.

Programs include:

  • Residential care homes for orphans
  • Emergency shelter for street children
  • Foster care support services
  • Family preservation and reunification programs

Extended Empowerment Programs

Building upon our core objectives, we offer extended programs that provide advanced skills and opportunities for youth empowerment through technology and innovation.

AI & Machine Learning

Free Basics Advanced

Introduction to AI, Python/R programming, and practical ML applications for solving community challenges.

  • Python programming for beginners
  • Data analysis and visualization
  • AI ethics and responsible innovation
  • Practical projects in agriculture and healthcare

Data Science & Statistics

Free Basics Certification

Data analysis, statistical modeling, and visualization skills for evidence-based community development.

  • Statistical analysis with R/Python
  • Data visualization with Tableau/Power BI
  • Survey design and data collection
  • Research methods for social sciences

Quantum Science Basics

Introductory

Introduction to quantum computing concepts and their potential applications for future technologies.

  • Quantum mechanics fundamentals
  • Quantum computing applications
  • Future career opportunities in quantum fields
  • Partnerships with research institutions

Academic Support

Free Support Mentorship

Dissertation guidance, university project assistance, and research mentorship for students in STEM fields.

  • Research methodology workshops
  • Thesis writing support
  • Statistical analysis assistance
  • Academic publishing guidance

Clinical Trials Training

Certification

Training in clinical research methodology, data management, and regulatory compliance for healthcare professionals.

  • Clinical research ethics
  • Data management and biostatistics
  • Regulatory affairs and compliance
  • Good Clinical Practice (GCP) certification

Agri-Tech & Entrepreneurship

Workshops Incubator

Combining agricultural innovation with entrepreneurship to create sustainable businesses and food security solutions.

  • Precision agriculture techniques
  • Agri-business development
  • Market access and value chains
  • Technology for sustainable farming

Target Beneficiaries

Our programs are designed to serve the following groups, with a special focus on marginalized communities in Zimbabwe:

Vulnerable Children

Orphans, street children, and those in difficult circumstances

Youth (15-35 years)

Unemployed youth, school leavers, and young adults seeking skills

Healthcare Professionals

Medical students, nurses, and clinical officers interested in research

Farmers & Agric Workers

Smallholder farmers and agricultural extension workers

University Students

STEM students needing research support and practical skills

Women & Girls

Special focus on empowering women and girls in technology

Service Areas
