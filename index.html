Ultimate Convenience

Experience unparalleled ease with our diverse payment options, including popular mobile money platforms like EcoCash, OneMoney, MyCash, and direct bank transfers via ZIPIT. Need more time? Seamlessly extend your parking session directly from your phone, eliminating the need to rush back to your vehicle. Our user-friendly platform means you can complete your parking transaction in just a few taps. Whether you're paying for an hour or purchasing a daily pass, the process is quick and intuitive, designed to get you on your way faster. Plus, with options to prepay for your parking, you can arrive in the city center with peace of mind, knowing your parking is already sorted.

Enhanced Security

Park with peace of mind knowing your vehicle is in a secure and regularly monitored environment. Our parking zones are equipped with strategically placed surveillance (CCTV in key areas) and patrolled by our highly visible, well-trained parking marshals. These dedicated personnel not only assist you with parking but also actively deter theft and vandalism, ensuring a safer parking experience for all motorists in Bulawayo. Your vehicle's safety is our priority.

Our commitment to security extends beyond just surveillance. Our marshals undergo regular training in customer service and incident response, ensuring they are prepared to handle various situations professionally and efficiently. This human element, combined with technological safeguards, creates a robust security net, significantly reducing risks and allowing you to focus on your activities in the city without worrying about your car.

Transparent & Affordable Rates

Tendy Three Investments offers clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees, making parking predictable and budget-friendly. For regular commuters and businesses, we also offer a range of cost-effective long-term and corporate passes, designed to meet diverse parking needs.

We understand that parking costs can add up. That's why we've structured our pricing to provide excellent value, whether you're a casual parker or a daily commuter. Our daily pass is particularly popular for those spending extended periods in the CBD, offering significant savings compared to hourly rates. We regularly review our pricing to ensure it remains competitive and fair, contributing to accessible urban mobility for everyone in Bulawayo.
