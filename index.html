Why Choose Tendy Three Investments Parking Solutions?

Ultimate Convenience

Experience unparalleled ease with our diverse payment options, including popular mobile money platforms like EcoCash, OneMoney, MyCash, and direct bank transfers via ZIPIT. Need more time? Seamlessly extend your parking session directly from your phone, eliminating the need to rush back to your vehicle. Our system links payments directly to your vehicle's registration number for a hassle-free experience.

Enhanced Security

Park with peace of mind knowing your vehicle is in a secure and regularly monitored environment. Our parking zones are equipped with strategically placed surveillance (CCTV in key areas) and patrolled by our highly visible, well-trained parking marshals. We are committed to deterring theft and vandalism, ensuring a safer parking experience for all motorists in Bulawayo.

Transparent & Affordable Rates

Tendy Three Investments offers clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Our flexible hourly rates cater to short visits, while our popular US$4 Daily Pre-paid Pass provides unlimited parking across all TTI-managed bays in the CBD for the entire day. We also offer various long-term passes and corporate solutions designed to provide cost-effective parking for regular users and businesses.

Your Trusted Local Partner

As Bulawayo's dedicated parking solution provider, Tendy Three Investments works in close partnership with the Bulawayo City Council under a Build, Operate, and Transfer (BOT) arrangement. We understand the city's unique needs and are committed to improving urban mobility, reducing congestion, and contributing to the city's infrastructure development and local employment. We are here to serve Bulawayo, for Bulawayo.
